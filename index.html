Anonymous Transactions on the Ethereum Network

Visor believes in the power of anonymity, in the security of privacy, in the inalienable right to freedom.

Focusing on advanced sorting algorithms, random seed generation techniques, and gas cost forecasting, our research aims to redefine the transactional journey in blockchain networks. Our goal is to ensure that every transaction is not only cost-efficient but also prioritizes user privacy and security.
